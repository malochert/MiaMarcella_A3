About

Join our popular 8-week Beginner’s wheel course at Slow Clay! Explore ceramics from clay preparation to glazing, blending Japanese-inspired techniques with ergonomic safety. Move from structured learning to personalized exploration, and take home your fired creations.

What’s Included?

The course fee at our studio covers everything you need, including all materials such as unlimited soft Midfire clay, glazes, and access to studio equipment and select tools. If you choose to have your pieces fired, there's an optional fee of $15 per kilogram, payable at the end of the term when you collect your fired work.
